Our Story

Built from struggle.
Designed for freedom.

Makilci was born from a real problem of porn addiction — the founders experienced firsthand how digital addiction quietly erodes focus, confidence, and connection. We combined behavioral psychology with modern technology to build a system that actually works.

🎯

Our Mission

Break Artificial Pleasure. Build Real Life.To help people worldwide reclaim control over their digital habits, attention, and ultimately — their lives. We believe technology should serve you, not the other way around.

🔭

Our Vision

A future where every person has the tools to build a healthier relationship with technology — distraction-free, present, and fully alive to what matters most.

📖

Our Story

Makilci started as a personal tool and grew into a platform. Today it serves users worldwide who are breaking free from digital cycles they never consciously chose.

🔬

Our Method

We combine cold-turkey blocking with gradual tapering, behavioral triggers, and positive reinforcement — a complete system, not a single trick.
